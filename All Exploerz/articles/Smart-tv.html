Key Features to Consider Before Buying

1. Display Technology

The display is the most important component of any television. QLED panels generally offer brighter images and more vivid colors compared to standard LED displays. If your budget allows, choosing a QLED model can provide a noticeable improvement in overall viewing quality.

2. Screen Size

Most buyers in Nepal choose between 50-inch and 55-inch televisions in this budget range. Larger rooms may benefit from 65-inch models, while smaller living spaces often feel more comfortable with a 50-inch display.

3. Operating System

A smooth operating system significantly affects daily usability. Google TV, Android TV, Tizen, and webOS are among the best smart TV platforms available today. Ensure that your chosen television supports popular applications such as YouTube, Netflix, Prime Video, and Disney+ where available.

4. HDR Support

HDR technology improves brightness, color range, and contrast. Televisions with HDR10 or Dolby Vision support generally provide a more immersive viewing experience, especially when watching modern movies and streaming content.

5. Audio Performance

Many modern televisions prioritize slim designs, which can sometimes limit speaker performance. If audio quality is important, consider models with Dolby Audio support or plan to pair your television with a dedicated soundbar.

Common Mistakes Buyers Make

Many consumers focus solely on screen size while ignoring picture quality and software performance. A larger television does not automatically provide a better experience if the display technology is outdated or the operating system feels sluggish.

  • Choosing size over picture quality.
  • Ignoring operating system performance.
  • Overlooking warranty support.
  • Not checking HDR compatibility.
  • Ignoring room size and viewing distance.
  • Buying based only on brand reputation.

Future-Proofing Your Purchase

A television is typically used for several years, making future-proofing an important consideration. Look for features such as 4K resolution, HDR support, Google TV or equivalent smart platforms, HDMI eARC, and strong software support. These features ensure that your television remains relevant as streaming services and entertainment technologies continue to evolve.

Additionally, if you are a gamer, verify support for low latency modes and modern HDMI connectivity standards. While premium gaming features may not be fully available under Rs. 1 lakh, many televisions now offer respectable gaming performance for casual users.
